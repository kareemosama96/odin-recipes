meat and egg tacos

INGREDIANTS

(6)..... eggs
(1/4)... cup crumbled cooked meat
(2)..... tbp butter
(3)..... slices American cheese
(1/4)... tp salt
(1/4)... tp ground blac pepper
(6)..... flour tortillas
(1)..... cup salsa

STEPS

  1. Whisk eggs together in a bowl; stir in meat.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add egg mixture; cook and stir until eggs are completely set,2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in American cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. Wrap tortillas in damp paper towels; microwave until warmed through, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  6. Spoon 1/4 cup egg mixture into the center of each tortilla; fold sides to cover. Serve with salsa.
